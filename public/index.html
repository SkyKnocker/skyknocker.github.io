About Me

This is going to be my digital portfolio! Have always been into the ‘How does this work?’ mindset. Took many things apart as a kid and some of those items made it back together and continued to work. Joined the Marine Corp right out of High School and Learned AC/DC theories, Electronics, HVAC, and Heavy Machinery Repair to sum it up. Finished my 4 year contract and got out. 6 months later realized I needed to go back in but did not like my options the Marines were given me and decided to join the Army. In the Unites States Army I was in school for more than a year Learning more Electronics. This time I was getting into Micro electronics, cable repair, Fiber optic connections and Fiber optic repair. A lot of schematics to look at and understand along the way. I got hurt in training which damaged my lower back(2009). Which got me medically retired from the Army(03/2010). Spent some time jumping from one bad job to the next and finally got my CDL and became a truck driver in 2014. I recently gave the over the road trucker lifestyle the boot. Decided to go back to school to feed my ‘How does this work?’ Mindset. Currently attending Full Sail University out of Winter Park Florida enrolled in their IT/Cybersecurity degree program. As of right now I have learned a lot, but only the surface of many subjects. Now to find that diamond in the rough job where I can stay and grow my skills and learn more.
